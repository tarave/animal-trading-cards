Orca

Orcas are the largest members of the dolphin family

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Length: 16 to 26 feet
  • Weight: 3 to 4 tonnes
  • Population: At least 50,000

Orcas are toothed whales which are found in oceans around the world. They have no natural predators and are highly social. Orcas are also known by the name Killer Whales.
